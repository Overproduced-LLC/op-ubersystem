Zeldathon Volunteer Agreement
  1. Code of Conduct and Marathon Rules

    I have read and understand the Zeldathon Code of Conduct and Marathon Rules. I agree to follow these policies at all times during the event. I understand that failure to do so may result in disciplinary action, including removal from the event and/or future event eligibility.

  2. Staff Direction

    I agree to follow instructions from Zeldathon staff and to fulfill my volunteer responsibilities to the best of my ability.

  3. Behavior and Representation

    I understand that while volunteering, I represent Zeldathon both on and off camera. I will conduct myself in a respectful, inclusive, and professional manner.

  4. Media Release

    I grant Zeldathon permission to record, photograph, and livestream my participation during the event. I understand that my voice, likeness, and image may be captured and used in event broadcasts, highlight reels, promotional materials, and social media. I waive any right to inspect or approve the finished product or its use.

  5. Voluntary Participation

    I understand that volunteering is optional and unpaid. I am participating of my own accord and may withdraw at any time, though doing so may affect my future eligibility as a volunteer.

  6. Infectious Disease Policy

    I understand that Zeldathon maintains health and safety protocols to reduce the risk of infectious disease transmission, including COVID-19. I agree to comply with all event health requirements, and adherence to masking/isolation procedures if I become symptomatic or test positive during the event. I acknowledge that I am required to have received the most recently released and CDC- or FDA-approved COVID-19 booster available to my age group prior to attending the event. I understand that these protocols may be updated based on conditions at the time of the event, and that noncompliance may result in removal from event areas.
