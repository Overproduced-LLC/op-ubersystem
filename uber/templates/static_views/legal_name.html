Name & ID Policy (Check-In, Privacy, and Administrative Use)

{{ c.EVENT_NAME }} is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment. To meet legal, safety, and logistical needs while protecting your privacy, we collect the following types of name information:

1. Legal Name (As shown on Photo ID)

Used only for internal verification at check-in and for bookings that legally require matching identification (e.g., air travel, lodging check-in with ID).

  • Only designated staff may view this name.
  • It will never be shared or printed on public materials
  • It is not used in day-to-day interactions

2. Badge Name (Public-Facing Name)

This is the name you'd like us to use for:

  • Your event badge
  • On-camera overlays and graphics
  • Discord nickname and general communication
  • Any printed or verbal recognition during the event
If your first and last names are exactly as they appear on your photo ID, you can simply check the box indicating this to skip filling in a separate name.

3. Administrative Name (Real name for Private Use)

Used when a full or commonly used real name is necessary for:

  • Travel or lodging bookings where ID may not be required
  • Emergency contact situations
  • Other administrative tasks where your badge name may not be sufficient or recognizable
This may be different from your legal name and is not used publicly. Only authorized staff will access this, and it will never be shared or used on materials.

Privacy Commitment

  • All name information is treated with strict confidentiality
  • Only the minimum necessary staff will see your Legal or Administrative Name.
  • Your names will never be shared across systems or with others without your consent.
