How do I sign up to volunteer at {{ c.EVENT_NAME }}?

All first-time volunteers must preregister for a badge. When you preregister, simply select the "Sign me up!" checkbox indicating that you wish to volunteer and you'll be contacted when shifts become available.

If you've already preregistered but didn't check the "Sign me up!" checkbox, then simply fill out your information on the login form on the Volunteer Login page, and you'll be presented with a link allowing you to volunteer.
